Features

Built for how you actually learn

Every feature is designed to make learning feel natural, rewarding, and something you want to come back to.

Explore Any Topic

Type anything you want to learn. Zensei generates personalized explanations tailored to your background, interests, and preferred learning style.

Adaptive Learning

Go deeper, simplify, ask follow-ups, or request examples and analogies. Every interaction adapts to how you learn best.

Gamified Progress

Earn XP, level up through ranks from Seed to Elder Tree, unlock achievements, and maintain daily streaks to stay motivated.

Learning Journal

Every topic you explore is saved with your ratings, tags, and notes. Track what you have mastered and what needs review.

AI-Powered Insights

Zensei learns your strengths and weak spots over time, giving you smarter explanations the more you use it.

Rate and Reflect

Rate your understanding after each topic. Your feedback shapes future explanations and tracks your growth over time.
