## Overview

Zensei is a personal study garden where you explore any topic and get explanations that feel like they were written just for you. It adapts to your role, education level, interests, and preferred learning style across every interaction.

A high school student asking about quantum mechanics gets a different answer than a physics graduate, and both get something useful. The more you use it, the better it understands how you learn.

**Try it now at [zensei.study](https://zensei.study)**

## Features

- **Explore Any Topic:** Type anything you want to learn and get a structured, personalized explanation instantly
- **Adaptive Responses:** Go deeper, simplify, ask follow-ups, or request analogies and examples on any explanation
- **Learning Journal:** Every topic is saved with full explanation history, ratings, and tags for future review
- **Gamified Progression:** Earn XP, level up through ranks, unlock achievements, and maintain daily streaks
- **AI-Powered Insights:** Zensei identifies your strengths and weak spots over time, shaping smarter explanations
- **Profile-Aware Context:** Onboarding captures your background, goals, and preferences to personalize from day one
- **Rate and Reflect:** Rate your understanding after each topic to track growth and guide future explanations
- **Depth and Intent:** Choose how deep you want to go and why you're learning, so every response matches your purpose

## How It Works

### Onboarding

When you first sign up, Zensei walks you through a profile setup. You tell it your role (student, professional, hobbyist), your education level, interests, age group, and how you prefer to learn (analogies, examples, visual, formal). This profile shapes every explanation you receive.

### Exploring Topics

Type any topic into the explore page. Zensei generates a structured explanation with sections:

- **TL;DR:** A quick summary to orient you
- **Core Explanation:** The main content tailored to your profile
- **Analogy:** A relatable comparison to something you already understand
- **Deeper Dive:** Extended detail for when you want more
- **Example:** A concrete, practical illustration

After reading, you can ask follow-up questions, request simplification, go deeper, or ask for more analogies and examples. Each follow-up builds on the conversation history.

### Learning Journal

Every topic you explore is saved to your journal with timestamps, tags, and your ratings. You can revisit past topics, see the full conversation history, and track what you've mastered versus what needs review.

### Insights

As you use Zensei, it builds an understanding of your learning patterns. It tracks what types of topics you gravitate toward, where you rate yourself low, and what explanation styles work best for you. This context feeds back into future explanations automatically.

## Progression

Zensei uses a gamification system inspired by RPGs to make consistent learning feel rewarding.

### XP and Levels

You earn XP for every topic explored, every follow-up asked, and every rating given. XP accumulates and levels you up, with each level requiring progressively more XP.

### Streaks

Maintaining a daily learning habit builds your streak. Active streaks provide XP multipliers:

| Streak | Multiplier |
|--------|------------|
| 1-2 days | 1.0x |
| 3-6 days | 1.2x |
| 7-13 days | 1.5x |
| 14-29 days | 1.8x |
| 30+ days | 2.0x |

### Ranks

Your rank reflects long-term growth and evolves as you level up:

| Rank | Level Range |
|------|-------------|
| Seed | 1-4 |
| Sprout | 5-9 |
| Sapling | 10-19 |
| Young Tree | 20-34 |
| Mighty Oak | 35-49 |
| Elder Tree | 50+ |

## Achievements

Achievements are unlocked by hitting milestones across multiple categories, with five tiers of rarity:

| Tier | XP Reward |
|------|-----------|
| Bronze | Base |
| Silver | Moderate |
| Gold | High |
| Platinum | Very High |
| Legendary | Maximum |

Achievement categories include:

- **Exploration:** Total topics explored
- **Curiosity:** Follow-up questions and deeper dives
- **Consistency:** Streak milestones
- **Mastery:** High ratings and demonstrated understanding
- **Depth:** Extended conversations on single topics

## Privacy

- All user data is stored in a private database tied to your account
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- Explanations are generated per-request and not shared across users
